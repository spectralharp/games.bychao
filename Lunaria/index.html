What is Lunaria?

Lunaria is a game about reuniting with old friends.

It's an action RPG with a combat system that focuses on combining the strength of different characters to overcome enemies with different weaknesses.

Features

  • Switch between 3 characters in battle from a total of 7 characters, each with a unique skill set.
  • Travel through a fantasy world with exotic, magical items that really makes you feel like a fantasy character, like pilk.
  • Attack and dethrone God?

The game tells the story of the traveling archeologist Sue, who is on an adventure to visit every temple of the Goddess Aria to uncover the truth behind how the moon shattered into a kajillion pieces. This may have led to her being wanted in several countries for trespassing private properties. Travel in a modern fantasy world where monsters are becoming increasingly hostile.
